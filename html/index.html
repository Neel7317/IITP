Electric Vehicles

COE-EV is the setup of the comprehensive state of the art Centre of Excellence (CoE) for Electric Vehicles (2W, 3W, passenger cars, buses and commercial vehicles) and their components such as traction batteries, motors, controllers, chargers etc. COE-EV offers clear advantages for hybrid/electric and autonomous vehicle manufacturers. COE-EV enables thermal management solutions for energy-dense batteries, powerful e-motors, and quick charging, and A better ride experience as vehicles become work and social environments, and Durable, upgradeable, and user-friendly charging stations.
